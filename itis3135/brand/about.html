About Us

Purple Trusting Hydra is a web design company that started up to help teach students basic HTML language. We offer interactive lessons to students and any person looking to learn HTML. We offer unique designs, allowing clients to request specific constraints to their websites. We will do our best to fulfill the client's desire; with any difficulties of the design we will contact the client and deal with the issues. We are willing to accept changes as the project moves forward, but the client must understand the affects it will have on the budget and deadline of the project.

Want a website? Here's what to do and expect:

We would love to create a webpage for any client seeking to start up a company! We ask that you consult with a representative of the company with your ideas. Website name, logo, images, etc that you may wish to include in your website. You will make an appointment when you reach out with our representatives. From the appointment, you will be able to talk to developers who will collect all information necessary to build a website. Please be ready to tell us about things such as: company name, point of interests, what pages you would like to have, format of webpages, etc.
