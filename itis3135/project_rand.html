Project Name: Quality Auto Washes Web Design

Project Overview:

This is a website application that presents the information and services of a car wash. The site will offer options of purchasing products provided by the company for home usage. The users of this website will be customers who wish to use car wash services or seeking products to wash their vehicle. There will be text and images to show the services of the car wash, such as package deals. There will be forms for the user to enter their information if they wish to contact the company.

Page Design

Home Page

  • Open and introduce the company to the visitor
  • Audience of this page is the customer
  • Introduction to the customers via text and pictures of clean cars
  • No data will be entered
  • No validation necessary
  • Buttons to other pages, such as Contact Us and About Us
  • If the user clicks on a button, the page will navigate to the corresponding page

About Us

  • Brief description of the company and history
  • Audience of this page is the customer
  • This page is a brief history of the company using text and images
  • No data will be entered
  • No validation necessary
  • Hyperlink to Contact Us page
  • Click on the link to navigate to another page

Contact Us

  • This papge will contain customer service numbers, emails, address of locations of Car Wash
  • Audience of this page is the customer
  • Content on this page will include text, forms, and buttons
  • Data input will be text and numbers

Careers

  • Audience of this page is the viewer

  • Data input will be text and numbers

Services

Shop

Dynamic Functionality on the Website

JavaScript will be used in various pages for different functionalities. For example, on the Home Page you will be able to click buttons that will open up a photo gallery of images of cars that have been washed. The Services Page will have a drop down menu where the user can choose different services, in which the page will load different content depending on which service was chosen. Shop Page will have the option to calculate the cost of the all items the customer chooses.
